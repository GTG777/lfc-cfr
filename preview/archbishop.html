ABOUT THE ARCHBISHOP

A Builder of Kingdom Vision

Archbishop Edgar Purnell serves as the visionary Founder and Presiding Prelate of Labourers For Christ Center For Reconstruction. With over four decades of dedicated ministry, his life's work has been characterized by a profound commitment to apostolic order, prophetic accuracy, and the tangible transformation of communities through the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

Born with a distinct calling to rebuild and restore, Archbishop Purnell has established a network of thriving ministries that span across denominational lines. His leadership style seamlessly blends the rich, regal traditions of the historic African American church with a modern, forward-thinking approach to corporate spiritual governance. He is renowned for his ability to translate complex theological truths into actionable strategies for daily living and organizational leadership.

Under his oversight, LFC-CFR has grown from a localized assembly into a robust center for spiritual reconstruction, offering robust theological training, community outreach programs, and global mission initiatives. His mandate remains steadfast: to equip the saints, restore the broken, and firmly establish the Kingdom of God on earth.
