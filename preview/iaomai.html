The word Iaomai is a Greek term meaning "to heal, cure, or make whole." At LFC-CFR, we believe that God's desire for His people is complete wholeness. True health is not merely the absence of disease, but a vibrant state of well-being across every dimension of our lives.

The Three Pillars of Wellness

Our wholistic approach addresses the complete person, providing resources and support for every aspect of your well-being.

church

Spiritual Wellness

Strengthening your connection with God through disciplined practices. This pillar focuses on prayer, fasting, and deep engagement with the Word to anchor your spirit in truth and divine purpose.

psychology

Soul Wellness

Nurturing your mind, will, and emotions. We provide resources for mental health awareness, emotional resilience, and inner healing, recognizing that a sound mind is crucial for holistic health.

fitness_center

Physical Wellness

Honoring the body as the temple of the Holy Spirit. This pillar offers guidance on nutrition, fitness, preventative health measures, and practical stewardship of your physical vessel.
