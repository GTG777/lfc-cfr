ABOUT THE MINISTRY

Standing in the Gap

The Wailing Wall Ministry is the intercessory heart of LFC-CFR. Inspired by the ancient Wailing Wall in Jerusalem - a place of raw, earnest, persistent prayer - this ministry team stands before God on behalf of individuals, families, communities, and nations.

Our intercessors are trained, faithful, and committed to confidentiality. When you submit a prayer request, real people who love God will be praying specifically for you and your situation.

"I sought for a man among them who should build up the wall and stand in the gap before me for the land."

- Ezekiel 22:30
